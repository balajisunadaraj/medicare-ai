Health Safety

Prevention & Safety Tips

Take proactive steps to prevent heart disease and cancer through lifestyle modifications and regular medical checkups.

🫀 Heart Disease Prevention

  • Maintain regular exercise (150+ min/week)
  • Keep blood pressure below 120/80
  • Reduce sodium and saturated fat intake
  • Manage stress through meditation
  • Quit smoking and limit alcohol
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Regular health checkups

🧬 Cancer Prevention

  • Maintain healthy BMI (18.5-24.9)
  • Avoid tobacco in all forms
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Increase physical activity
  • Eat more vegetables & fruits
  • Protect from UV rays
  • Regular cancer screenings

✨ General Wellness

  • Stay hydrated (8+ glasses/day)
  • Practice good mental health care
  • Maintain social connections
  • Regular health monitoring
  • Follow balanced diet patterns
  • Avoid processed foods
  • Annual medical examination
